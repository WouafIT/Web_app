Post a Wouaf is simple, follow the tips below:

A Wouaf is an event published for a limited period. You can post all kinds of events in Wouaf IT.

Here's a little help:

  • Write a message of 300 characters maximum. Be brief, but do not overuse abbreviations to stay clear.
  • The title is optional, but helps to be clearer in the lists of Wouaf.
  • Add a category from the list. Choose well to be easily found.
  • You must give a start date and a duration for your Wouaf. It will be visible only for this period.
  • Your Wouaf will be positioned on the map at the location you have chosen.
  • You can add up to 3 pictures maximum.
  • You can add themes using a hashtag. This a word starting with #. Example: #car
  • You can quote a person with a @ followed by his pseudonym. Example: @wouafit

Tips and additional warnings:

  • Before you publish your Wouaf, reread it well because you can not change it.
    In case of error you will need to delete it and then recreate it.
  • Beware of personal informations you publish (address, phone number, etc.). Wouaffers can always contact you via email with Wouaf IT.
  • Attention Wouaf IT does not guarantee financial transactions. To avoid unpleasant surprises, we recommend that you always hand deliver things.
  • Remember to respect the terms of use of the service.
